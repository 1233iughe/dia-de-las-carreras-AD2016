Class Project for Aug - Dec 2016

Here we will describe the project for the class: web development laboratory - semester August December 2016

Main idea

The main idea for the class is to work with new web technologies such as: Koa.js, Aurelia, RethinkDB, Horizon, among others. These with the sole purpose of letting the students understand how to work with new and evolving technologies. While keeping the structure of a well-designed product; by taking requirements from the client going through the software development process, project management, quality assurance (testing) and delivery.

The project

Okay explain me, what are they doing?

The main idea is actually very simple; We took the following case scenario: This is a small company with limited budget trying to fix their inventory problem as well as human resources at the same time as they have to upload files, and have roles management system all this will also have to deal with invoices stamped by the government and sync with google calendar.

This allowed for the students to take requirements; setting goals for the project in a realistic manner.
